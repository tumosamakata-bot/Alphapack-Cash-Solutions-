Answers

Every question, answered.

Straight answers. No corporate language. No fine print buried at the bottom.

Who qualifies for a loan? ▾
You must be currently enrolled at a recognised educational institution in Botswana and hold a valid Student ID. Alpha Pack v5 serves students only. Employed non-students and community members are not eligible at this stage — a broader product is in development.
How much can I borrow? ▾
Four packages are available in Pula:

• Starter — P100 to P500
• Boost — P500 to P1,000
• Advance — P1,000 to P2,000
• Premium — P2,000 to P5,000 (may require additional review)

You select the exact amount within your chosen package.
What is the interest rate? ▾
25% for loans above P1,000. 30% for loans P1,000 and below. Interest is calculated once on the principal — it is not compounded. The complete total repayable amount is shown to you on-screen before you submit, every single time.
What are the repayment options? ▾
You may choose to repay in 14 days, 21 days, or 30 days. The due date is calculated from the date of approval. Repay as early as you like — the interest amount is fixed regardless of when within the window you settle.
Why must I verify my Student ID? ▾
Verification confirms you are a real, enrolled student — it protects you, other borrowers, and Alpha Pack from fraud. It's a one-time process per account. Once approved, you never have to redo it unless your status changes.
How long does approval take? ▾
Student ID review: 1–2 business days. Loan applications: typically the same business day they are submitted. You will see your status change in real time on your dashboard the moment an admin acts.
Can I apply without being logged in? ▾
No — and this is intentional. You must register, verify your ID, and log in before any application is accepted. This protects the integrity of the process for every borrower on the platform.
What photos do I need for verification? ▾
A clear photo of the front and the back of your physical Student ID card. Ensure all text is legible, lighting is even, and the full card is visible. You can upload image files or use your phone camera directly in the verification page.
What happens after I submit an application? ▾
Your application enters our review queue. An admin reviews your details — amount, purpose, contact number, and verification status. You track the outcome (Pending → Approved / Declined) live on your dashboard. No waiting by phone.
How do I contact Alpha Pack? ▾
WhatsApp or call +267 76 807 549 or +267 78 322 911. We're available Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 17:00. Have your student account details ready. We aim to respond to WhatsApp messages within 2 hours during working hours.
Is Alpha Pack a legitimate lending service? ▾
Yes. Alpha Pack Cash Solutions is an active, operational lending business in Botswana. Loans are real money. Interest is real. Repayment is enforced. Only apply if you genuinely intend to repay within the agreed window — defaulting has consequences.
