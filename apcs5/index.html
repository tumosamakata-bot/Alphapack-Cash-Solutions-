FAQ

Questions students ask us most.

Who can apply? ▾
Currently enrolled students at recognised institutions in Botswana. You must be able to produce a valid Student ID. Non-students are not eligible at this stage.
How much can I borrow? ▾
Between P100 and P5,000 depending on your package: Starter (P100–P500), Boost (P500–P1,000), Advance (P1,000–P2,000), Premium (P2,000–P5,000). Premium may require additional documentation.
What is the exact interest rate? ▾
25% for loans above P1,000. 30% for loans P1,000 and below. This is flat — calculated once on the principal. Total repayable is shown to you before you submit.
Can I apply without logging in? ▾
No. You must register and verify your Student ID before any application is accepted. This protects you, us, and every other borrower on the platform.
How long does approval take? ▾
Same business day in most cases. ID verification takes 1–2 business days. Loan review is typically completed the same day it is submitted.
